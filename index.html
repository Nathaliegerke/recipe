Swedish Pancakes

By: Nathalie Gerke

Fresh And Crispy Pancakes Like No Other!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2,5 dl milk
  • 2,5 dl flour
  • 4 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 lemon
  • 40 gram butter
  • A pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Start with mixing together the flour, salt, vanilla and the zest from the lemon in a large bowl.
  2. Add half of the milk in the bowl and stir and then let that rest.
  3. Take another bowl and whisk the eggs porous. Mix this with the other bowl
  4. Melt the butter and then add this in the bowl.
  5. Add the rest of the milk and let the paste rest for 30 minutes.
  6. Now fry the pancakes on a hot frying pan. Use some butter for the first 2-3 batches
